Migration Overview

How to Read This Table

This calendar summarizes the typical whale migrations visible from the Big Sur South Coast. It focuses on approximate size, pod behavior, and when different species are most likely to be seen from shore.

Legend

  • Season shows the main months whales are commonly observed.
  • Distances are rounded annual travel ranges for each species.
  • Pod size notes whether whales are usually solitary, in pairs, or in larger groups.
