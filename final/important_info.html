This is a Dangerous Place

Big Sur is a ribbon of asphalt clinging to the edge of the continent — a narrow, windy road carved into steep cliffs that plunge straight into the Pacific. The views are unreal, the kind that make your brain forget about gravity, speed limits, and the fact that your car is still moving. And that’s the trap. Those breathtaking moments become split-second distractions with very real and sometimes deadly consequences

Safe Practices

  • PULL OVER!! It's normal to want to stare off into the vastness of Big Sur. That's what you're here for. Just don't try to do it while you're driving. Lingering in the roadway will cause an accident behind you.
  • See something you like? PULL OVER!!
  • Intimidated by the curves or the people behind you? PULL OVER!!
  • Does your passenger want to take pictures or videos of the scenery? PULL OVER!!
